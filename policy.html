Mental Health and Environmental Education Initiative

Policy Option 1: Environmental Educational Mandates

Implementing mandates for environmental education that focuses on the benefits of nature for mental well-being and sustainable practices.

Policy Option 2: Green Spaces and Urban Planning

Mandating a certain percentage of land within each district for greenery and requiring hospitals to provide nearby greenspaces.

Policy Option 3: Mandated integration of Ecopsychology and Eco-therapies

Integrating eco-therapies and ecopsychology principles into behavioral and mental health practices.
